Sunflower

The meaning of sunflower is the sunflower itself. The sunflower is a symbol of confidence, stability, one love, one heart and has the meaning of beautiful art. If you receive a sunflower, it is like receiving a message that “No matter how arrogant you are, But someday I will win your heart" and also means “My love is strong and always loyal to you. Just like a sunflower that never looks at anyone except the sun."
